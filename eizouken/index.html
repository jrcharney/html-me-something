Keep Your Hands Off Eizouken!

About

Keep Your Hands Off Eizouken! is a Japanese manga series written by Sumito Ōwara about three high school girls who start a Motion Picture Club (lit. 「映像研」 or "Eizouken") where they make animated films. They distinguish themselves from the school's Anime Club since Asakusa wants to be creative, Mizusaki is forbidden from joining the Anime Club by her parents, and Kanamori wants do something that makes money.

With Asakusa's imagination and ability to draw creative scenery, Mizusaki's ability for drawing figures and design, and Kanamori's shrewd but rational production skills, the girls create amazing features and slowly work towards making their "greatest world".

The manga became an anime that premiered in 2020 and has also been made into a movie in Japan.

Characters

Midori Asakusa

Asakusa is a first-year student who likes anime. She is full of curiosity and imagination, but is not very sociable. She has the ability to draw settings based on her daily life but with a twist of creativity. Her family moved to the town of Shibahama when she was young. Often she is seen wearing a camoflage boonie hat. Her backpack is also military surplus which may indicate that her father is in the military.

Tsubami Mizusaki

Mizusaki is a young actress who wants to be an animator someday, but her parents want her to pursue modeling and acting like they have. As such, she was forbidden from joining the school's Anime Club, but thanks to Asakusa and Kanamori's teamwork, she become the third member of Eizouken. Mizusaki is famous for her appearances in advertizing and film, but she wants to do something else with her life.

She has an eye for figure drawing which allows her to create animated characters that complement Asakusa's settings.

Her affluent lifestyle often makes her naïve to the cost of things, which is why Kanamori generally reign in the cost of things.

Sayaka Kanamori

Kanamori is a tall, shrewd girl who rarely smiles. But behind her scowl is a rational, smart young woman eager to pursue making money. Her ability to manage time and money make her the club's leader and often the person who wrangles Asakusa and Mizusaki and acquire the resources the club needs.

She has a backpack that looks like a pair of Converse Chuck Taylor shoes.

Any conflict the group has with other clubs, the Student Council or the School Staff she usually handled with a bit of crafty manipulation.
